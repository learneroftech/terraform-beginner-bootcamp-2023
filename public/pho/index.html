Making my own Pho

Authentic Vietnamese Pho Recipe (Serves 12-16)

Ingredients for Broth:
1 Large White Onion
5-6 Medium Shallots
Ginger (large chunk or several small pieces)
2 lbs Quality Beef Bones
16 QT Pot
Small Cinnamon Stick (secret ingredient)
1 Box of "pho Hoa"
1 Box of "Vien gia vi Pho"
8-10 Star Anise
24 oz Fish Sauce

Ingredients for Bowl:
4 lb Chuck Roast or Bottom Round Roast
5 lbs Fresh Rice Noodles ("Banh Pho Tuoi")
Optional: Cilantro, Green Onions, Mung Bean Sprouts, Sriracha, Hoisin Sauce, Lime Wedges

Steps:
1. Preheat oven to 450Â°F. Roast peeled onion, shallots, and ginger until browned (10-15 mins).
2. Boil beef bones in 16 QT Pot filled with water. Add roasted veggies to the pot.
3. Add all broth ingredients except fish sauce to the pot. Simmer on medium-high heat.
4. Add beef roast to the pot. Reduce heat to medium. Cook until tender (2.5-4 hours).
5. Prepare garnishes (cilantro, green onions, lime slices) while the roast cooks.
6. Once meat is tender, remove and cool. Strain broth, removing all solids.
7. Add fish sauce to broth for flavor. Adjust to taste.
8. Serve hot. Boil fresh noodles separately (2-3 mins). Assemble bowl with broth, meat, garnishes, and noodles.

Tips:
- Use quality beef bones for best flavor.
- For leftover broth, cool and refrigerate to remove fat layer easily.
- Feel free to customize garnishes to your liking.
